The importance of artificial intelligence 1- Preserving the accumulated human experiences and transferring them to smart machines 2- Using the human language with machines instead of computer programming languages, which makes the machines accessible to all segments of society, even those with special needs, after dealing with advanced machines was restricted to specialists and experts. 3- Helping in diagnosing diseases, prescribing medications, and interactive education 4- Smart systems will contribute to the areas in which decision-making is made. These systems enjoy independence, accuracy and objectivity, and therefore their decisions are far from wrong, bias, racism, prejudices or even external or personal interference. 5- Smart machines will relieve a lot of people, risks, psychological pressures, and a temptation that focuses on more important and humane things. This will be done by employing these machines to do difficult and dangerous work, exploring unknown places and participating in rescue operations during natural disasters. These machines will also have an effective role in fields that include many details that are characterized By complexity, which requires tired mental focus, continuous mental presence, and sensitive and speedy decisions that cannot tolerate delay and error.
